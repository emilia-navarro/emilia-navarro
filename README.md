# Emilia Navarro

Independent Balkanist and author at [MagiaAlbanii.pl](https://magiaalbanii.pl/autorzy/emilia-navarro/).

I write about Albania, Balkan studies, regional history, cultural heritage, routes, legends, local stories and less obvious places beyond the main tourist trail.

## Areas of interest

- Albania and the Balkans
- Balkan studies
- Albanian history
- Cultural heritage
- Local history and legends
- Regional travel guides
- Hiking routes and cultural landscapes
- Editorial maps and structured travel content

## Current work

I contribute to MagiaAlbanii.pl, an Albania-focused travel and cultural guide built around regions, hubs and satellite articles.
